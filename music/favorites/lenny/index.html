lenny - stevie ray vaughan

when asked about my favorite song of all time, lenny by stevie ray vaughan is typically the answer that i give. my mom first showed me this song when i was early in high school and getting deeper into my teenage rock phase. i had just started to learn guitar and was obsessed with jimi hendrix. little wing was always on repeat. this combined with my moms recollections of seeing srv live shortly before his death made for a quick attachment.
